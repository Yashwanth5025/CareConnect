Our Mission

Who We Are

CareConnect is a registered NGO dedicated to making quality healthcare accessible to underprivileged communities. We connect patients in need with trained volunteers, doctors, and mental health professionals — all at zero cost.

🩺

Medical Assistance

Access free consultations, prescription guidance, and referrals to government hospitals in your area.

🧠

Mental Health Support

Connect with trained counselors and mental health advocates for anonymous, judgment-free support.

💊

Medicine Aid

Our medicine bank programs ensure essential medications reach those who cannot afford them.

🚑

Emergency Response

Rapid volunteer dispatch for health emergencies, with coordination support for ambulance services.

Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find your answer? Ask our AI-powered assistant below.

Is CareConnect completely free? ▾
Yes. CareConnect is a non-profit initiative. All patient support services, volunteer matching, and resources are provided at zero cost.
How quickly will I be matched with a volunteer? ▾
For non-emergency cases, we typically match within 24–48 hours. Emergency requests are expedited and handled within a few hours based on availability.
Who can register as a volunteer? ▾
Anyone with healthcare-related qualifications (MBBS, nursing, pharmacy, counseling, etc.) or community outreach experience can apply. We conduct a brief screening for medical roles.
Is my personal data safe? ▾
Absolutely. Your data is encrypted and stored securely. We never sell or share your data with third parties. You may request deletion at any time.
What areas does CareConnect currently serve? ▾
We currently operate in 48 cities across India. Telehealth services are available nationwide. We are actively expanding to rural areas.
Can I get mental health support anonymously? ▾
Yes. Mental health sessions can be taken under a pseudonym via our anonymous support pathway. We respect your privacy completely.
